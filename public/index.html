A National Strategy to Safeguard and Strengthen the MLS

Ally MLS is acquiring select MLSs nationwide — focusing on those ready to de-risk and operate independently from association or broker ownership. We offer them a path to retain their identity, leadership, and staff while joining a connected national network.

Our model keeps what works for members and consumers, modernizes operations, and frees MLS leadership to run like a business — without having to get buy-in from dozens of competing interests.

The timing is urgent. Legal pressures are mounting, industry fragmentation is accelerating, and the tools agents rely on are falling behind. Ally MLS creates a united front to preserve cooperation, protect data integrity, and keep the system working for the people who depend on it most.

About David Anderson

David Anderson has spent his career turning vision into reality — building companies that transform industries and create lasting value.

He led the internet division at Avioa, helping the company evolve from a traditional brick-and-mortar operation into a global player before its sale. He went on to found, scale, and sell LionDesk, one of real estate’s leading CRM platforms, and has advised and invested in multiple high-growth startups across technology and real estate.

At Ally MLS, David’s mission is clear: safeguard the integrity of real estate data and ensure it serves the people buying and selling homes — not the companies trying to wall it off. By bringing MLS leaders together and building a stronger, more connected network, he’s working to preserve cooperation as the engine that delivers the best outcomes for professionals and consumers alike.

Advanced Tools, Zero Disruption

When Ally acquires an MLS, nothing changes overnight for brokers or agents. The systems they use remain in place — no retraining, no lost productivity.

In parallel, we begin offering optional, next-generation AI capabilities — the same class of tools that have powered Lundy’s products for years — to enhance search, listing input, compliance, and member support.

These tools work alongside existing MLS platforms, giving agents and brokers the choice to adopt them at their own pace. Our approach ensures modernization without disruption — technology that adapts to the MLS, not the other way around.
